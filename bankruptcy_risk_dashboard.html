Senior Data Scientist Assessment — Strategic Findings
🔴 Top Risk Finding
Borrowing Dependency is the #1 bankruptcy predictor (5.2% importance), followed by Net Income/Assets and Total Debt/Net Worth. Critical-tier companies show a 99.4% bankruptcy confirmation rate — these are near-certain failures already in motion.
⚠️ Opportunity Zone
The High-risk tier (82 companies, 58.7% bankruptcy rate) represents the highest-value intervention window. These companies are distressed but salvageable. Targeted equity injection combined with interest rate relief can reduce this cohort's bankruptcy expectation by ~23%.
✅ Model Confidence
Ensemble AUC of 0.953 confirms excellent discrimination. The model correctly identifies 94%+ of bankruptcies in validation. Gradient Boosting marginally outperforms Random Forest (0.953 vs 0.933). Class-balanced training corrects for the 97:3 imbalance.
